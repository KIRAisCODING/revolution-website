About

Human connection works with you embracing every aspect of your life. Despite challenges, there is a belief that we all possess the ability to empower and regain control of our lives. Through pain we grow, finding reasons to love ourselves and accomplish the impossible. We are Revolution Coaching and Counseling and your host is: Ojie Okosun (Mr. Revolution).

We are a certified and experienced Business, Relationship, and Life Coaching and Counseling Service. Together with our team of experts, we can help your relationship and marriage stay fulfilled.

Our unique Coaching & Counseling skills are unrivaled which is founded in our vast knowledge of identifying problems & Conflict Resolution, Emotional intelligence, Anger, Control & Dominance concerns, Love language, Problems related to family/interpersonal & workplace relationships, boosting productivity & relationship in Business & Workplace, Parents & kids, student & teacher relationships makes us a top choice.

Mr. Revolution is a member of the International Association of Professional Organizations (IAPO). Within his career, he has had the privilege of working with couples, singles, families, schools & business organizations who have had challenges, conflicts in dealing with both relationships within the home, schools and in the business environment.

We strongly believe just as we teach that "a healthy Relationship is the bedrock of happiness & productivity"; hence it is important to build & maintain one.

As experts with in-depth knowledge and understanding of human relationship, business, career paths, human needs, desires, connection and behavioral pattern, we have designed a roadmap towards guiding you with the aim of resolving issues that affect your relationships. Over the years, we have developed techniques and strategies towards achieving the desired positive outcome to issues & conflict resolution.

As experienced, unbiased and patient professional, we will walk and work with you all through your difficult journey. Working together, understanding the problem within, will help us achieve the desired goal together.

It is my mission to offer professional relationships & business skills that will help achieve healthy common interests, inter personal goals, strategy & profitability in business.
